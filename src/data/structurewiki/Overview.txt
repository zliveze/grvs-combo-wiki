<div class="home-card home-card--col4 home-card--row1" bis_skin_checked="1"><div class="home-card-title-large" bis_skin_checked="1"> Overview</div>
Charlotta is a rushdown character who excels at applying pressure at close range using various advancing attacks with high damage to boot. Her quick jumps are both strong in offense and defense, allowing her to overwhelm the opponent and get out of situations nimbly. These strengths make Charlotta excellent at capitalizing off the smallest mistakes and snowball her opponents at a momentâ€™s notice.<div style="clear:both" bis_skin_checked="1"></div>

<p><span style="white-space: nowrap;"><span class="charaLabel"><span class="mw-default-size" typeof="mw:File"><a href="/w/GBVS/Charlotta" title="GBVS/Charlotta" bis_skin_checked="1"><img alt="" src="/wiki/images/thumb/4/46/GBVS_Charlotta_Icon.png/28px-GBVS_Charlotta_Icon.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="28" height="28" class="mw-file-element" srcset="/wiki/images/thumb/4/46/GBVS_Charlotta_Icon.png/42px-GBVS_Charlotta_Icon.png 1.5x, /wiki/images/thumb/4/46/GBVS_Charlotta_Icon.png/56px-GBVS_Charlotta_Icon.png 2x"></a></span></span><span style="visibility:hidden;"> </span><span style="white-space: normal;"><a class="mw-selflink selflink"><b>Charlotta</b></a></span></span> is a rushdown character with plenty of tools to pressure the opponent at close range.
</p>

<div class="Pros-Header" bis_skin_checked="1">Pros</div>
<div class="Cons-Header" bis_skin_checked="1">Cons</div>
<div class="Pros" bis_skin_checked="1">
<ul><li><b>Above average damage:</b> With a lot of corner carry, the ability to use two specials in one combo even midscreen, and an easier time achieving successful jump-ins, Charlottaâ€™s damage output is generally higher compared to the rest of the cast.</li>
<li><b>Strong pressure and okizeme:</b> She has many ways to pressure and stay in thanks to advancing normals and specials such as <a class="mw-selflink-fragment" href="#Sword_of_Lumiel" bis_skin_checked="1">Sword of Lumiel</a>. On knockdowns, her <a class="mw-selflink-fragment" href="#Noble_Strategy" bis_skin_checked="1">Noble Strategy</a> brings a variety of ways to mix and open your opponent up which gets especially dangerous in the corner.</li>
<li><b>Awkward to fight:</b> Her fast jumps and burst range forces your opponent to play patient and react quick or get snowballed in a matter of seconds. Additionally, her reversals and supers can be awkward to punish. She also has a tendency to fall out of combos that would work on most others in the cast thanks to her fall speed.</li>
<li><b>Good jumps:</b> Her fast fall speed means anti airing Charlotta is harder than most. The shorter jump-arc also makes her cross-up game stronger. Characters who canâ€™t rely on anti airing with a reversal can get overwhelmed quick, especially once you bring her <a class="mw-selflink-fragment" href="#Noble_Strategy" bis_skin_checked="1">Noble Strategy</a> into the mix.</li></ul>
</div>
<div class="Cons" bis_skin_checked="1">
<ul><li><b>Somewhat stubby normals:</b> Most other characters have pokes that outrange Charlottaâ€™s pokes, with the exception of her <span class="colorful-text-4">f.H</span> which is too slow to be reliably used in neutral.</li>
<li><b>Slower reversal:</b> <a class="mw-selflink-fragment" href="#Holy_Ladder" bis_skin_checked="1">Holy Ladder</a> is one of the few slower, non-metered reversals in the game. It is susceptible to being low-profiled and as a result allows other characters to pressure her easier, especially those who can play around both weaknesses.</li>
<li><b>Struggles vs zoning:</b> Since she has no fireball of her own, she has trouble dealing with projectiles in general.</li>
<li><b>Awkward anti-airs:</b> Her <span class="colorful-text-4">2H</span> is very slow. This means she often has to rely on other tools to anti air like <a class="mw-selflink-fragment" href="#Holy_Ladder" bis_skin_checked="1">Holy Ladder</a> or <span class="colorful-text-2">5U</span> which generally net her less reward, or use <span class="colorful-text-4">c.H</span> which is a proximity normal that only triggers if the opponent is close enough.</li></ul>
