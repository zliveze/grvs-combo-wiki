<div class="attack-info-body" bis_skin_checked="1">
<p>Charlotta winds up and launches herself forward with a multi-hit attack.
</p>
<ul><li>Invulnerable on startup.</li>
<li>Useful for punishing fireballs and other whiffed moves on reaction.</li>
<li>More hits will land the closer you are.</li>
<li>Awkward to punish on block for characters with stubby normals since Charlotta will bounce back a bit. Can be spotdodged after the super flash for a closer punish, but the timing is strict as it only leaves her -8.</li>
<li>Technical input launches the opponent less, leading to better oki if you hit it midscreen.</li></ul>
