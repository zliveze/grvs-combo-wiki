<div class="attack-info-body" bis_skin_checked="1">
<ul><li>Takes 30 frames to charge.</li></ul>
<p>A fast and advancing charge move that bounces off the opponent on hit or block. Charlotta’s go to special cancel to confirm hits from her pokes. Thanks to the fast nature of Shining Onslaught your opponents will be forced to play around the move, meaning they will either take it slow, position themselves at a distance where they can safely react to it, or risk getting hit by it. Though once it’s on cooldown that threat is gone and Charlotta loses the ability to confirm most of her pokes without spending meter, making her neutral a lot less threatening. Additionally, very fast on whiff.
</p>
<hr>
<dl><dt><span class="colorful-text-1">[4]6L</span></dt></dl>
<ul><li>Short travel distance.</li>
<li>Safe on block.</li>
<li>Leaves standing on hit.</li>
<li>+4 on counterhit (crouching counterhit will combo into <span class="colorful-text-1">c.L</span> in the corner)</li>
<li>Doesn't bounce her as high after hitting the opponent, meaning it avoids Cagliostro's <span class="tooltip" aria-expanded="false" tabindex="0">Air Mehen<span class="tooltiptext"><span class="mw-parser-output"><span class="tmp"><span class="tmp-image"><span typeof="mw:File"><a href="/w/File:GBVS_Cagliostro_236X_Set.png" class="mw-file-description" bis_skin_checked="1"><img src="/wiki/images/thumb/6/6d/GBVS_Cagliostro_236X_Set.png/130px-GBVS_Cagliostro_236X_Set.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="130" height="129" class="mw-file-element" srcset="/wiki/images/thumb/6/6d/GBVS_Cagliostro_236X_Set.png/195px-GBVS_Cagliostro_236X_Set.png 1.5x, /wiki/images/thumb/6/6d/GBVS_Cagliostro_236X_Set.png/260px-GBVS_Cagliostro_236X_Set.png 2x"></a></span></span><span class="tmp-items"><span class="tmp-item"><span class="tmp-item-label">Guard</span><span class="tmp-item-data">Mid</span></span><span class="tmp-item"><span class="tmp-item-label">Startup</span><span class="tmp-item-data">39</span></span><span class="tmp-item"><span class="tmp-item-label">Recovery</span><span class="tmp-item-data">Until L+4</span></span><span class="tmp-item"><span class="tmp-item-label">Advantage</span><span class="tmp-item-data"></span></span></span></span></span></span></span><link rel="mw-deduplicated-inline-style" href="mw-data:TemplateStyles:r403431"> unlike the other two versions.</li></ul>
<p>It’s 0 on hit, but since you’re usually more ready for it than your opponent it’s often still your turn afterwards.
</p>
<hr>
<dl><dt><span class="colorful-text-3">[4]6M</span></dt></dl>
<ul><li>Long travel distance, but slower startup.</li>
<li>Knocks down on hit, and wallbounces on counterhit.</li>
<li>Punishable on block by most of the cast. Some can always punish, for others it’s spacing dependent, while a few can’t punish it at all unless they block it in the corner.</li></ul>
<hr>
<dl><dt><span class="colorful-text-4">[4]6H</span></dt></dl>
<ul><li>Long travel distance.</li>
<li>Safe on block.</li>
<li>Gives a hard knockdown and wallbounces.</li>
<li>Confirms from all your pokes at most ranges.</li></ul>
<p>The version you usually want to confirm into, as landing it means you are now in and can start pressuring your opponent from up-close.

<div class="attack-info-body" bis_skin_checked="1">
<p>Charlotta’s invincible reversal and another charging move in her kit. A rising attack that comes back down with another attack. With 11 frames of startup it is one of the few slower reversals in the game, but on the positive side it’s harder to punish than most others as it pushes your opponent out and is only -7 or -8 if crouch blocked (higher if stand blocked) meaning your punishment for using it on block tends to be lower than average. Bigger punishes are possible, however easier to mess up. 
</p>
<ul><li>Good anti air option. Easy input can be used for reaction anti airs when you weren’t charging it beforehand.</li>
<li>Good combo ender. Especially <span class="colorful-text-4">[2]8H</span> as it takes a while to finish allowing your cooldowns to come back. It also leads to easy safejumps afterwards to keep your offense going.</li>
<li>Technical input launches the opponent less which means hitting <span class="colorful-text-4">[2]8H</span> allows for cross-up safejumps, making it very strong when landing it while you’re cornered.</li></ul>
