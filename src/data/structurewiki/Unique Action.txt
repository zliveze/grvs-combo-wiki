<div class="attack-info-body" bis_skin_checked="1">
<p><span class="colorful-text-2">5U</span> consists of two parts, a Parry and a Counter that automatically follows up if the opponent is close enough.
</p><p><b>Parry:</b> Parries all moves on frames 6-23 except for throws, (Super) Skybound Arts or OD activation, and reduces the incoming damage by 50%. The damage <i>is</i> affected by <a href="/w/GBVS/Damage#Guts" title="GBVS/Damage" bis_skin_checked="1">Guts</a>, but is <i>not</i> considered <a href="/w/GBVS/Damage#Chip_Damage" title="GBVS/Damage" bis_skin_checked="1">chip damage</a> so if you don't have enough HP to take the attack you will still die. 
</p><p><b>Counter:</b> If the opponent is close enough when their move is parried, a Counter slash automatically activates for 1500 damage, a side switch, and a hard knockdown. If your opponent is out of range for the Counter to activate, they get staggered leaving you slightly plus but only when using moves with strike property. Parried projectiles at a distance will not affect your opponent.
</p>
<ul><li>Fairly reliable anti-air, as it is fast and will trigger even against jump normals from far above or behind you.</li>
<li>Do not bother using it vs fireballs at a distance where the Counter doesn’t trigger, since you’re just taking unnecessary damage and can still be punished afterwards.</li>
<li>With 6 frames of startup it is slower than <span class="colorful-text-1">c.L</span>, so not recommended to prioritize when you need something to mash out of pressure.
